OkCupid Drug Data

A series of charts analyzing the differences between 45,866 Bay Area OkCupid users that reported using drugs often, sometimes, or never.

Anonymized raw data collected by Albert Y. Kim and Adriana Escobedo-Land with permission in June 2012, accessed by Angelica Walker on Feburary 8th, 2017 via ERIC.

It is important to note that self-reported data may lead to inaccuracies. However, this data provides a strong picture of how users wish to be percieved by potential dates.
